How It Works

My Simple 4-Step Process

From your raw footage to a publish-ready video — a clear, stress-free workflow every time.

01
Brief & Files

You share your raw footage, references, and creative direction. I'll ask the right questions to understand your vision completely.

02
First Cut

I deliver a first edit with structure, pacing, and style locked in — for your initial review and feedback.

03
Revisions

We refine based on your notes. Up to 3 revision rounds included — until you're genuinely happy with every frame.

04
Final Delivery

Export in your preferred format. Clean files delivered via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or your platform of choice.
